Website Evaluation: University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC)

Website Information

URL: https://www.umgc.edu

Name: University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC)

Target Audience

Prospective and current students, military personnel, working adults, and faculty.

Site Organization

The UMGC website is structured with a top navigation menu that includes sections for degree programs, admissions, tuition, and student resources. It has a search bar and well-organized drop-down menus. Which I say is designed rather well.

CRAP Principles

Contrast: The red, yellow and white theme of the website helps to give a "certain" pop of colors against the white background.

Repetition: Consistent layout.

Alignment: Well-structured text and images.

Proximity: Relevant links are grouped together logically. (in a way that makes sense)

Accessibility Audit Score

78%☹️ (let's not talk about that).

Effectiveness

The site provides clear pathways for admissions, degree programs, and financial aid information.

Efficiency

Users can quickly find information via search and navigation menus.

Engagement

The design is professional and visually appealing, with testimonials and success stories.

Recommendation for Improvement

From my experience, interacting with the webpage on my mobile device(android) it is rather "slow" so I would say opitmizing the webpage on mobile phones is necessary and would be highly appreciated (but it could also just be me).
