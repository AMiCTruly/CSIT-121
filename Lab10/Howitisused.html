How is TTML IMSC1 Used?

TTML IMSC1 is used to author and render subtitles and captions for internet-delivered media. It supports multiple text formatting features such as font size, alignment, positioning, and styling to ensure clarity and consistency in text display.

Example Usage

  <tt xmlns="http://www.w3.org/ns/ttml">
    <head>
      <styling>
        <style xml:id="s1" tts:fontSize="12px"/>
      </styling>
    </head>
    <body>
      <div>
        <p begin="00:00:01.000" end="00:00:04.000" style="s1">
          Hello, world!
        </p>
      </div>
    </body>
  </tt>

This example displays a subtitle reading “Hello, world!” from 1 to 4 seconds into a video.
