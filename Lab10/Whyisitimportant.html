Why is TTML IMSC1 Important?

TTML IMSC1 is important because it provides a unified, interoperable standard for delivering captions and subtitles across different platforms and devices. It improves accessibility for people who are deaf or hard of hearing and enables localization of video content through translated subtitles.

It is also a key component for regulatory compliance in countries where subtitles and captions are mandated for online video. The profile ensures reliable rendering of timed text, making media accessible and inclusive.
