What is TTML IMSC1?

TTML IMSC1 (Timed Text Markup Language Internet Media Subtitles and Captions 1.0) is a W3C specification that defines a standard profile of TTML1 and TTML2. It is designed for internet-delivered subtitles and captions, supporting worldwide accessibility and media interoperability.

IMSC1 simplifies the complexity of TTML by specifying a limited set of features from TTML1 and TTML2 that are most widely needed for subtitle and caption presentation in internet-delivered media.
